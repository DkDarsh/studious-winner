Examinations For Engineering

Government Exams:

JEE MAINS
It is a national exam conducted by NTA

It is taken by almost 15 to 16 lakh students across 10 days in January and April. Number of students is increasing every year due to which competition increases massively.

Almost 2.5 lakhs students qualify this exam and are allowed to write JEE ADVANCE paper which allows students to get admission in IIT and other reputed institution

JEE ADVANCE
It is also a national exam for the students who qualify JEE MAINS and is conducted by JAB

It is the qualifier exam for IIT and other national colleges like IIST, RGIPT and IIPE.

Around 29,000 students qualify this exam out of which only 17,900 get seats in IIT

Private Exams:

BITSAT
It is private exam conducted by BITS Pilani

It is one of the most reputed exam after JEE MAINS and JEE ADVANCE.

It allows admission to BITS colleges around India. Special exam is conducted for admission in Dubai campus

VITEEE
It is a private exam conducted by VIT VEllore.

It is also a very famous college and ranks high in India

Comedk
It is a private exam conducted by the unified body of all the colleges in Karnataka.

It allows admission to all engineering and medical colleges in the state.

Engineering Colleges in India

There are countless engineering colleges in India.

Government Colleges Using JEE MAINS

National colleges and state government colleges offer admission using JEE MAINS rank to students. These colleges offer HOME STATE QUOTA.

These colleges are alloted using JOSAA(Joint Seat Allocation Authority)

NIT: National Institue of Technology
There are 31 NITs in India.

These colleges offer HOME STATE quota to the students of the state the college is located in. The seat quantity in HS quota is managed by the state government.

IIIT: Indian Institue of Information Technology
There are 26 IIITs in India.

These colleges too offer HS quota.

GFTI
There are around 47 GFTIs in India.

These Colleges are funded by the government and offer HS quota.

Government Colleges Using JEE ADVANCE

IITs, RGIPT, IIST, IIPE offer admission using JEE ADVANCE ranks

These colleges are alloted using JOSAA(Joint Seat Allocation Authority)

IIT: Indian Institue of Technology
There are 23 IITs in India.

These colleges are the considered the elite and top most colleges in India.

RGIPT: Rajiv Gandhi Institue of Petroleum Technology
There are 3 branches in India.

These colleges are the premier institues for petroleum research and allied subjects.

IIST: Indian Institue of Space Technology
There is only 1 IIST situated in Trivandrum

This college specializes in space research.

IIPE: Indian Institue of Petroleum Engineering
There is only 1 branch of this college situtated in Andhra Pradesh.

This college too specializes in petroleum industry and its allied branches.

General

OBC NCL

Other backward classes non creamy layer, backward classes earning less than a specified income.

OBC CL

Other backward classes creamy layer, backward classes earning more than a specified income.

SC and ST

Schduled Castes and Tribes, ver backward classes as specified in the Indian Contitutuion.
